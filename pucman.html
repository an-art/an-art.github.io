How to play

Arrow keys to move Pucman (up/down/left/right).
Don't bump into sumo. Simply score as many points as you can eating the small rices all around the maze.
1 point per rice (201 of them).
Big points come when you eat 1 of the 8 Sushi worth 10 points located in different parts of the maze.
You can gather a possible 281 points to win the game.
